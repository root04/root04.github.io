--- layout: post title: "enabling minimize/maximize button on gnome3" date: 2011-12-11T00:21:00-08:00 comments: false published: false ---
gconftool-2 --set /desktop/gnome/shell/windows/button_layout --type string close,minimize,maximize:

or use gconf-editor.which ever you like.

Logout->Login, there you have it.
BTW, if you want the buttons on the right top,the right string is ":close,minimize,maximize".
